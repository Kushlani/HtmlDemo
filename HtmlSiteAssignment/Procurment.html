Procurement Guidelines

What is procurement?

Procurement involves the process of selecting vendors, establishing payment terms, strategic vetting, selection, the negotiation of contracts and actual purchasing of goods. Procurement is concerned with acquiring (procuring) all of the goods, services, and work that is vital to an organization.



Procurement Methods

  • International Competetive Bidding (ICB)
    •

    ICB shall be used when the capacity of the domestic contractors,suppliers and service providers are limited

  • National Competetive Bidding (NCB)
    •

    NCB is the competetive biding procedure that shall be generally applicable for most Sri Lankan Government Funded projects

  • Limited International Competetive Bidding (LICB) & Limited National Competetive Bidding (LNCB)
    •

    These procedures are followed when the invitation to bid is directly addressed to a pre-selected ist of international suppliers or contractors. The procedure is chosen when only a few sources are avaialable.


    Minimum period of bidding time shall be maintained as stated below
